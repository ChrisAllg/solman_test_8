Business Web Portals and Apps with Intrexx

What is Intrexx?

In Intrexx you can easily design intranet pages and create applications for presenting, connecting, managing and using your company data in unlimited ways.

Live demo Personlig demo Features

Intrexx allows you to quickly create a powerful business portal to accelerate your company. Its flexibility allows for unlimited possibilities; integrate data from virtually any source and create all sorts ofapps to present, manage and use that data on the site.

With just some work inthe user-friendly design tool, you can create features for your data usually only found in “tailor made” intranets and mobile apps. This flexibility can also give an easily put together Intrexx solution a longer lifespan than tailor made ones, which usually require more expertise and work to update with new functions or additional data sources. With Intrexx Share, the portal is complemented with an extensive social platform which empowers users with social collaboration around the company data.

What does Intrexx do?


Intrexx allows you to quickly create a powerful business portal to accelerate your company. Its flexibility allows for unlimited possibilities; integrate data from virtually any source and create all sorts of apps to present, manage and use that data on the site.

With just some work inthe user-friendly design tool, you can create features for your data usually only found in “tailor made” intranets and mobile apps. This flexibility can also give an easily put together Intrexx solution a longer lifespan than tailor made ones, which usually require more expertise and work to update with new functions or additional data sources.

Personal portal

The whole portal experience changes depending on who the user is:
Get the content and knowledge you need for your work.
View and manage your relevant documents, clients, etc.
Responsive design allows for fully featured mobile use.

Intrexx Share

An extensive social platform complements the portal:
Collaborate across departments.
Seek out contact people and experts within the company.
No clutter, only relevant information in your feed.
All the company’s knowledge is now searchable.

Easy, low-code platform

Creating in Intrexx is easy, even with minimal coding knowledge:
Drag-and-drop, “What you see is what you get” design tool.
Integrate what you will as an application.
Built-in store with ready-to-use applications and templates.

Other functions

More features that make Intrexx extremely versatile:

Integrate any data and establish connections between them.

Automate processes.

Manage users and permissions on the site.
